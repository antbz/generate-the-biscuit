Cocoa powder and coconut flour cookies with toasted pumpkin seeds and chocolate glaze

  • Beginner Level
  • 40 Mins
  • 8 Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 158 g coconut flour
  • 262 g cocoa powder
  • 62 g light brown sugar
  • 68 g shortening
  • 83 ml coconut milk
  • 67 g toasted pumpkin seeds
  • 51 g chocolate ganache
  • 54 g chocolate glaze
Watch the video Watch the video

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. In a bowl, whisk coconut flour and cocoa powder until combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat light brown sugar with the shortening until light and fluffy.
  4. Add coconut milk to the mixture and beat on high speed until combined.
  5. Slowly incorporate the dry mixture into the wet mixture and beat until combined.
  6. Cover dough and chill in the fridge for 1 hour.
  7. Form desired amount of balls of dough and place on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Decorate with toasted pumpkin seeds, chocolate ganache and chocolate glaze.
  11. Enjoy!
